Clinical Practice Guidelines
This directory contains standardized clinical protocols, treatment guidelines, and best practice 
recommendations from authoritative medical organizations.

Example Data Types:
Specialty society guidelines (ACC, ASCO, IDSA, etc.)
Government health agency protocols (CDC, WHO, NHS)
Hospital-specific treatment protocols
Clinical pathway documents
Evidence-based medicine summaries

Example File yaml:
guideline_id: "IDSA-2024-PNEUMONIA-001"
title: "Management of Community-Acquired Pneumonia in Adults"
organization: "Infectious Diseases Society of America"
version: "3.0"
publication_date: "2024-01-15"
last_review_date: "2024-01-15"
next_review_date: "2027-01-15"

key_recommendations:
  - category: "Diagnosis"
    recommendations:
      - "Chest radiography recommended for all patients with suspected pneumonia"
      - "Blood cultures recommended for patients requiring hospitalization"
      - "Sputum cultures for patients with severe disease or risk factors for MRSA"
  
  - category: "Treatment"
    severity_stratification:
      - outpatient_no_comorbidities:
          first_line: "Amoxicillin 1g three times daily for 5 days"
          alternative: "Doxycycline 100mg twice daily for 5 days"
      - outpatient_with_comorbidities:
          first_line: "Amoxicillin-clavulanate 875mg twice daily PLUS azithromycin 500mg daily"
          alternative: "Cephalosporin PLUS macrolide"
      - inpatient_non_icu:
          regimen: "Beta-lactam PLUS macrolide"
          duration: "5-7 days"

  - category: "Risk Assessment"
    tools:
      - "CURB-65 score for mortality risk"
      - "PSI/PORT score for admission decisions"
      - "qSOFA for sepsis identification"

quality_indicators:
  - "Time to first antibiotic dose less than 4 hours"
  - "Blood cultures obtained before antibiotics"
  - "Appropriate antibiotic selection based on risk factors"
